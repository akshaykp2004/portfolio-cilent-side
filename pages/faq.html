The FAQ

How long does a bespoke commission take?

Generally, we require 4 to 6 weeks for a bespoke commission. During peak seasons (like Royal Ascot or Wedding season), we recommend booking 8 to 10 weeks in advance to ensure availability.

Do I need to visit the studio in person?

While we love meeting our clients at our Shoreditch atelier, we offer highly successful virtual consultations worldwide. We can guide you through taking your own measurements and discussing designs via video call.

How do I know my hat size?

During your consultation (virtual or in-person), we will provide specific instructions on how to measure your head correctly. For bespoke pieces, we create the hat to your exact millimeter measurements for a perfect fit.

What is your shipping policy?

We ship worldwide using premium courier services. All hats are securely packed in our signature HMS hat boxes, designed to protect the delicate millinery during transit. Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and the size of the box.
