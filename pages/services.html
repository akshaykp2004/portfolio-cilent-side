The HMS Standard

Signature Elements

We believe the soul of a hat lives in its fibers.

Parasisal

Hand-woven from agave fibers, our parasisal straw offers an ethereal sheen and structural resilience.

Beaver Felt

The gold standard of millinery. Our felts are sustainably sourced and blocked to a velvet-smooth finish.

HMS Silk

Hand-dyed in small batches at our studio, ensuring every ribbon and bloom holds a unique, vibrant depth.

Antique Lace

Sourced from French heritage archives, adding a whisper of history to modern silhouettes.

The Journey

The Bespoke Timeline

From first inspiration to the final unveil, a masterpiece takes time.

I. Consultation

We discuss your silhouette, event, and inspirations, selecting initial color swatches and materials.

II. Blocking

Initial hand-blocking on vintage forms. The first 'naked' shape is created for your appraisal.

III. Trimming

Hand-stitching of ribbons, flowers, and ornaments. This is where the hat’s unique soul is sculpted.

IV. Final Unveil

The final fitting and steam. Your piece is hand-packed in our archival hat box for delivery.
